What Are Temporary Plates?

Temporary plates, also known as temp tags or temporary permits, are provisional authorizations issued by the DMV (Department of Motor Vehicles) that allow drivers to operate a vehicle while completing the paperwork to obtain permanent plates. Temporary plates are essential for situations such as purchasing a new vehicle, transfers of ownership, or vehicles that have not yet passed inspection.

In 2026, obtaining DMV temporary plates is easier and faster than ever, especially with the services available ONLINE. What Are Vehicle Insurance Policies?

Vehicle insurance policies are fundamental contracts that provide financial protection against losses caused by accidents, theft, or damage to third parties. In 2026, having valid insurance is not only a personal safety measure but also a legal requirement in most states to legally operate your temporary or permanent plates.

Through our platform, you can immediately verify the validity and type of coverage of your policy. It is vital to understand that there are different levels of protection designed to meet the needs of each driver and the regulatory requirements of the DMV.

We primarily manage two types of coverages in our records:

  • Liability (Responsabilidad Civil): It is the mandatory basic coverage. Covers material damage and injuries that you may cause to other people in an accident
  • Full Coverage (Cobertura Completa): Includes Liability plus protection for your own vehicle against collisions, theft, vandalism and natural disasters.
